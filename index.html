Elemental Attack

A mythical dragon (Abrugog), who everyone thought to be dead, was captured by the souls of the damned after the great battle. With the city of the living getting tormented by the souls of the dammed, the people of the city must decide between a life of slavery or death. The McCane family chose to strike a deal with the souls of the dammed to save themselves from this life by offering their children to the high priest, condemning their children to a fate worse than death. Damned. The siblings had no hope or chance of survival until an old woman from a neighbouring village took pity on them as she couldn’t watch them be condemned to this fate. A flash of lightning struck them where they stood supercharging them with powers from the gods. Lilly the youngest of the siblings was gifted water manipulation form Poseidon god of the sea while Roman was gifted the wrath of the Vulcans fiery grasps. And together they will embark on a quest to save Abrugog and restore the world back to everlasting peace and tranquillity.

As the player you must find the 20 feathers of Abrugog then present the feathers to the high priest to enter the boss fight. These feathers could be hidden anywhere therefor the player must look around the map carefully to find them. The souls of the damned will chase you and if they get too close then explode draining your life away. The boss fight consists of the player avoiding the dragon's fiery breath while collecting ammo. This ammo can be used by water girl on the crossbow to kill the possessed Abrugog and bring peace to the city.

Planet Wars

Planet Wars is a game where humans are fighting back against the impending doom of an alien race making the player humanity’s last hope to survive. The player must eradicate all the aliens before the aliens can blow up the world.

This game uses a realistic gravitational pull around the moon to create a challenge for the user. Combining this with a boost mechanic makes for a fun player experience.

Naval Underwater Scavanger MK 33

I was tasked with building some naval tech and I wanted to make somthing unique that still met the theme.

I picked the colour blue so the drone would blend in with the environment. I used red to show the deadly end of the guns too as red is usually associated with death or blood. I decided to add arms to the drone so it could retrieve items from the underwater missions as shown above in the gif.

I made the drone in 3DS max and then imported it into Unity to make the shader and background scenario.

Battle Of the ages

This mobile game uses everything that I have learned and more. This includes:

  • Money counter
  • Vendors
  • Skeleton killers
  • Missile Evasion sequence
  • Killing frogs
  • Invisible maze
  • Western shootout
  • End Cut scene
The vendors have many features throught the game:
  • Drop potions of healing
  • Reveal the path of the hidden maze
  • Slow the missiles
  • Grant the player ammo
  • Open doors
  • Make the zombies chase the ai rather than the player
I spent around 2-3 weeks to build this game as I wanted to create something special. The only flaws are when optimising the game even with frustrum culling and occlusion culling the game requires a high spec phone to run it however pc controls have been implemented too.

Nightmare Survivor

Nightmare survivor is a horror concept with a simple aim of not letting the lights turn off. If the lights turn off it is game over. As the player you must repair 3 generators to survive but don’t forget to power the lights as you also need to manually crank the lights to keep them on. Can you survive?
